Digital Closet

A command-line tool that turns your event, season, budget, clothing size, and shoe size into outfit ideas and clickable shopping links (Zara, H&M, Zalando, Jack & Jones, and more).

GitHub Repo Download binaries

Example

digital-closet recommend wedding winter men --size m --shoe-size 42 --accessories --budget-max 200

Features

  • Fast CLI input (event/season/gender/sizes/budget).
  • Generates store search links for Germany + EU.
  • Works offline for recommendations; links open in your browser.

How it works

Digital Closet builds a keyword query from your inputs, then generates official store search URLs where possible and safe “site:” search links for other brands.

Pricing idea

Free for personal use. Future “Pro” could add saved profiles, more brands, and smarter matching.
